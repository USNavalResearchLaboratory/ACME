4.4.3. Acknowledgement Packet¶

class acme.EmissionObj.AckPacket(theSourceID, theDestID, theTime, thePayload, theMsgID)[source]¶

Bases: acme.EmissionObj.Packet

Implements a specific type of information unit to be transmitted by a CommsPlatform to acknowledge the receipt of a Packet from another CommsPlatform.

The destID and payload of this object are the sourceID and msgID, respectively, of the Packet that was successfully received.
